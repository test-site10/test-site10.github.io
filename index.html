Mothership

what we do

Mothership is a collective of artists and performers. We make ensemble based theater in the moment, through improvisation and collaboration. We weave together moments and characters to make thoughtful comedic works of art.

Don't worry, we're not as pretentious as that sounds. Our shows are mostly just good old fun and silliness, but we pride ourselves on keeping artistry alive in our work.
